# PiHelm Privacy Policy — Changelog

All material changes to the PiHelm privacy policy are recorded here.
Versioning follows semver: `MAJOR.MINOR.PATCH`.

## [1.0.0] — 2026-05-04

Initial version. Released alongside PiHelm v1.0 on the App Store.

### Highlights

- Declares zero collection of personal data on external servers
- Documents all local storage locations (UserDefaults, Keychain, SwiftData, iCloud KVS)
- Documents the 7-day Pro trial mechanism and how to reset it
- Documents the SSH connection model (peer-to-peer, no intermediate servers)
- Documents background refresh behavior for alerts
- Bilingual (English / Spanish)
